What is Presence House?

Presence House is a physical third place—neither home nor office—where architecture encourages presence and fewer screen-first habits. The first launch city is Carlsbad, CA, beginning with our Phase 1 donation campaign.

Why it matters

In an age of hyper-connectivity, we’ve never felt more isolated. Presence House is a return to the room we’re actually in.

Frequently Asked Questions

A clear look at how Presence House works, who it is for, and how the space helps people spend less time on screens.

What is Presence House, exactly?

Presence House is a modern third place: a physical social club designed for calm, meaningful connection. Think boutique café energy, library focus, and community club warmth under one roof.

What is your screen-use goal inside Presence House?

Our goal is simple: help people spend less time looking at screens while in the building, and more time in conversation, community, and the room itself.

What kinds of things happen inside the house?

Members can join silent reading hours, open conversation tables, community dinners, deep work sessions, analog creative hours, and themed social gatherings with light structure and a warm host culture.

How does the companion app fit in?

The app stays lightweight for check-in and scheduling, then gets out of the way so people can spend less time on screens inside the building.

What is the fundraising campaign for?

The current campaign funds architectural design, buildout, furnishings, and launch operations for the first Presence House location. Current campaign goal: $250,000.

What membership pricing are you exploring?

We are testing multiple pricing tiers to keep the club high-quality and sustainable. The Founding List form lets you share your preferred range so we can design plans that balance accessibility and excellence.

Where will the first Presence House open?

Our first planned location is in Carlsbad, California.

What phase is the project currently in?

We are currently in Phase 1: collecting donations to fund design, buildout, and launch prep for Carlsbad.

What does Phase 1 funding pay for?

How much are you trying to raise in Phase 1?

Why start in Carlsbad?

Carlsbad aligns with the calm, community-first culture we want to build and is the strongest starting point for the first Presence House chapter.

Will there be more cities after Carlsbad?

Yes—Carlsbad is intended as the first flagship. Future expansion depends on successful launch and member demand.
